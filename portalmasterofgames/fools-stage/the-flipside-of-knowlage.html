About the book

A road through journals, archives, and forgotten places

Alias Lupus discovers a mysterious traveling archive and a coin connected to ancient Structures scattered across the world. Alongside Maris, Rowan, Corvin, and Eldric, he follows a trail of journals, unanswered questions, and forgotten places toward a truth that may change how he understands himself.
