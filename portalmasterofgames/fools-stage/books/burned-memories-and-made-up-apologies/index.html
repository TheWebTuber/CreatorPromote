About the book

A road through old scars, impossible warnings, and unfinished words

Alias Lupus continues along a road that remembers more than it should. As strange warnings, half-buried memories, and uneasy apologies begin to surface, the journey forces him and his companions to face what was lost, what was hidden, and what still needs to be said.
