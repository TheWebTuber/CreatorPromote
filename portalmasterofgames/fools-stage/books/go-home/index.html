About the book

The final road, the final door, and the choice waiting at the end

Alias Lupus and his companions reach the end of a road that has carried them through archives, memories, warnings, and impossible truths. But going home is not only about returning to a place. It is about deciding what to carry forward, what to forgive, and who they are after everything the journey has changed.
